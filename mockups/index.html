Logical Flow Summary

Office Day
  1. Quote uploads via Hub → job appears with all budget items grouped + units expanded
  2. Open job → Overview shows the budget bar + grouped items already populated
  3. Review Units tab → confirm per-unit budgets, divide any job-level items if needed
  4. Assign supervisors to job in Setup → they see it on their phone instantly
  5. Watch claims roll in from site app, see budget bars update live
Site Day
  1. PIN login → only assigned jobs shown
  2. Tap job → 4 tiles + Form 12 tile
  3. Log Cost → all dropdowns pre-filled with last-used → Save (works offline)
  4. Take photos as evidence — geotagged automatically
  5. Update progress slider → invoicing webhook fires → office sees % update
  6. Flag issues with photos → office can promote to variation/rework
End-of-Day
  1. Office checks Reports — sees over-budget alerts immediately
  2. Promotes any flagged items into Variations or Rework as needed
  3. Adds PO/invoice numbers to claims (in collapsible Accounting section)
  4. Form 12 outcomes recorded → certs uploaded
  5. Budgets close out, job marked complete, install_status flips
